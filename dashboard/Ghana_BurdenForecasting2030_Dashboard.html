National indicator trends and 2030 forecasts

Dashed segment: ARIMA point forecast to 2030. Full methodology in the manuscript (Figure 1).

Order-selection sensitivity, all 21 series

AICc difference between each series' own selected order and a uniform ARIMA(1,1,1) refit. Only 1 of 21 series matched the uniform order exactly; 11 of 21 were statistical near-ties (manuscript Table 4).

Structural-break sensitivity

Testing rather than assuming away the 2020 COVID-19 and 2022 Ghana currency-crisis breaks: a decisive currency-crisis break shifts the malaria 2030 forecast by a further +26.5% (larger than the order-selection effect above), and a decisive break at both candidate dates is found for the World Bank life-expectancy series -- a plausible partial explanation for its divergence from the WHO-GHO life-expectancy series, not a resolved causal account. Most series' break tests are underpowered on 2–5 post-break observations (manuscript Table 5).
